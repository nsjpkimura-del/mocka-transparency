# MoCKA Transparency

Verification and Proof Layer of the MoCKA Insight System

---

## Philosophy

MoCKA is not a system that merely generates AI outputs.

MoCKA transforms AI outputs into **verifiable research artifacts**.

Knowledge requires three properties.

Traceability  
Verifiability  
Provability  

Transparency exists to guarantee these properties.

---

## Dual Movement Architecture

![Dual Movement](docs/images/mocka_dual_movement.svg)

The MoCKA system maintains knowledge circulation through two movements.

Primary Movement  
Normal research and verification pipeline.

Shadow Movement  
Fallback research path maintaining a minimum operating capability of 75 percent.

---

## Verification Architecture

![Verification Architecture](docs/images/mocka_verification_architecture.svg)

The Transparency layer converts AI output into verifiable evidence.

AI Output  
↓  
Research Artifact  
↓  
Cryptographic Evidence  
↓  
Public Verification

---

## Workflow

1 AI experiment produces an output  
2 Artifact is generated and stored  
3 Hash and signature are produced  
4 Evidence becomes publicly verifiable

---

## Role in MoCKA Ecosystem

MoCKA Core  
Produces research results.

Knowledge Gate  
Preserves institutional memory.

Transparency  
Transforms results into publicly verifiable evidence.
