# Code of Conduct

## EN

MoCKA is a governance-focused engineering project.
We value clarity, evidence, and respectful collaboration.

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical substance and reproducible claims.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, and disruptive behavior.

## JP

MoCKA は統治を扱う工学プロジェクトです。
明確さ、根拠、相互尊重を重視します。

- 敬意と建設性を保つ
- 技術的実体と再現可能な主張を優先
- 嫌がらせ、個人攻撃、妨害行為をしない
